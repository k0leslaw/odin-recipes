Toast

After completing this recipe, you will have perfect toast.

Ingredients

  • two slices of bread of your choice
  • butter (optional spread after cooking)

Steps

  1. plug in toaster
  2. place each slice of bread into the toaster
  3. set the dial to the desired cooking time
  4. push down the lever
  5. when done, remove toast carefully so as to not burn yourself
  6. place on a plate and enjoy!
