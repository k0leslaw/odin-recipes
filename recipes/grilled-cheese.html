Grilled Cheese

After completing this recipe, you will have the perfect grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • two slices of bread of your choice
  • two slices of pepperjack cheese
  • butter (to grease pan and cook sandwich in)

Steps

  1. heat up pan on medium heat for about five minutes
  2. put enough butter in the pan to coat the bottom
  3. put both pieces of bread in the pan until the side facing the pan begins to turn golden brown
  4. remove the pieces of bread onto a plate, assemble the grilled cheese (golden side inside)
  5. put more butter in the pan, and put the sandwich in. Flip when golden brown on the side facing the pan
  6. Cut diagonally and enjoy!
